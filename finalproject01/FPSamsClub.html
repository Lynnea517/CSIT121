Sam's Club

Home Giant Target Sam's Club
Pros Cons Personal Opinions
  • Bulk items are their specialty. You can find everyday essentials in bulk quantities.
  • They have a wide selection of food products. Sam's club sells frozen items, baked items, produce, and pre-pakaged meals.
  • Membership comes with a wide range of bonuses and benefits.
  • Members can shop on line and have the option to do delivery or pick up.
  • You have to be a club member in order to shop there
  • Membership is expensive. A Sam's Club membership, which is the standard membership, is $50/year. A Sam's Club Plus membership is $110/year.
  • Items are mostly sold in bulk quantity. There aren't too many items from Sam's Club that are sold in a regular quantity.
  • I like Sam's Club for the items that me and my household really like or when I'm in the mood to make groceries last for 3-4 wks without making any additional trips.
  • Sam's Club sells bulk items at really decent prices and $100 - $150 can go a long ways in this store.
  • They have just about everything from entrees and side dishes, to snack and desserts. The bakery is a plus if your a dessert places. They typically have a wide range of freshly made desserts in stock.
  • The membership is pricey but your membership can be shared between your household or friends if you'd like. Depending on how many people are in your household you can have them contribute and help with the cost of the membership.
  • Sam's club is a great option for families. If you primarily cook meals at home and like to pack lunches throughout the week the bulk items will allow you to do that
