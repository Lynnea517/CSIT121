Pros Cons Personal Faves
  • Giant has quality meat and produce
  • Giant offers store buyers a bonus card free of charge. The bonus card allows shopers to take advantage of deals and sales.
  • Giant has a bakery and a deli and bakery inside their stores. Perfect if you prefer to buy items fresh.
  • Giant offers home delivery and curbside pickup for groceries.
  • Customer service is poor at times.
  • Only 166 stores throughout the US.
  • Recent issues with shoplifting have resulted in Giant either closing stores or decreasing the amount of products they keep in stock.
  • Giant is open for 6am to 10pm. I personally have had to make late night grocery runs and its nice to have store options that are open late night.
