Home Giant Target Sam's Club
Pros Cons Personal Faves
  • There are approximately 2,000 stores conveniently located throughout the US.
  • Target offers online shopping with the option for delivery or in-store pickup.
  • Target offers price matching. If you find an item in another store that has a lower price, Target will match that price.
  • Stores are typically clean and well-organized and the staff is very helpful.
  • Produce and meat quality is not the best.
  • I cant speak for all Targets but the ones I frequent have a Starbucks inside. Its nice to have the option to grab a quick cup of coffee while shopping.
